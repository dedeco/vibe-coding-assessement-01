How to Use Condominium Analytics

A step-by-step guide for beginners

1Getting Started

Welcome to Condominium Analytics! This application helps you analyze your condominium's financial data by asking questions in natural language. No need to learn complex commands or formulas.

2Check Connection Status

Look at the top-right corner of the screen. You should see a green dot with "Connected" status. If it shows "Connecting..." or "Error", wait a moment or refresh the page.

3Ask Your First Question

Use the text input at the bottom of the screen to ask questions about your expenses. You can ask in plain English, just like talking to a person.

Examples:
• "How much was spent on electricity last month?"
• "What are our maintenance costs?"
• "Show me all payments to CEMIG"

4Use Example Questions

If you're not sure what to ask, click on any of the example question buttons in the welcome area. These will give you ideas of what the system can help you with.

5Review Your Results

After asking a question, the AI will analyze your data and provide a detailed answer. Results may include numbers, charts, or explanations depending on what you asked.

6Use the Sidebar

The sidebar on the right shows your recent questions and quick action buttons. Use these to quickly repeat common queries or access frequently needed information.

7Ask Follow-up Questions

You can ask follow-up questions to get more details about any result. For example, after asking about total expenses, you could ask "Which vendor had the highest cost?"
